1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our practices. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information and what data practices we follow.

By using this application, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We want to be completely transparent: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users.

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information

  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

2.2 Local Storage

The only data stored is:

  • Game settings preferences (sound on/off, game difficulty level)
  • These settings are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences
  • This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party

3. PERMISSIONS

Our application does NOT request or require any device permissions, including:

4. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, process, or share any user data.

The local settings stored on your device are used solely to:

  • Remember your sound preferences
  • Save your selected difficulty level (Easy Bot, Normal Bot, Hard Bot)
  • Enhance your gaming experience

5. BACKGROUND SERVICES

Our application uses a local foreground service to provide background music during gameplay. This service:

  • Runs only when the app is active
  • Does not collect any data
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • Automatically stops when you exit the app
  • Does not require any special permissions
